About this Guy

I am currently a freelance illustrator from Atlanta, Georgia, specializing in
character design, concept art and sequential illustration.

I have worked with a variety of clients in the music industry, as well as my own
creator owned projects. I have an interest in game design and development, and
that is where my passion for programming comes from. I've started quite a few
projects in the Unity game engine, and quickly familiarized myself with the base
fundamentals of programming, as well as a general understanding of C#.

Through this course, I hope to better grasp my understanding of programming as a
skill, as well as expanding my knowledge of a variety of programming languages.
